World's Grumpiest RPG

What is that?

Have you ever played EarthBound? It was one of the best RPG games of its time. You watch Game Grumps? Well, if you didn't, you probably wouldn't be here. So how about we take those two things and merge them together? And so begins GrumpBound - a Grump-themed RPG based off EarthBound, where the chosen four - Arin, Dan, Jon and Ross save the world from Giygas, the universal cosmic destroyer.

Stay updated

As much as we try, the game is not yet ready for release. It is in early alpha stage, this is what we've done so far, and the first playable demo is expected to be released within a few months. Follow this project's official sites on social media to stay constantly updated about any progress made on the game. Any future updates and information will be posted on our Facebook page, as well as this website right here.

Support

GrumpBound will be free to play. But here's a list of things that aren't free - food! Whether the game will ever be finished, or whether or not will it be a fun experience, it all depends on your support. Everyone can do something to help us. Share the information about this project to all lovelies you know. Leave a positive comment to motivate us. After the project reaches its beta stage, we might start accepting donations.

We need you!

If you have any assets that would help us make this game happen, please join the team! Are you good at writing or level design? Or perhaps you'd like to work on the concept art? Any spriters, graphics designers, composers? Whatever you do, your work will surely help advance the project. As a member, you will have access to early game versions and your efforts will be mentioned in the credits. Visit the joblist for more information.
