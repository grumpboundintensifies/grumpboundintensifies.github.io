Earthbound was a great game. Game Grumps are great people. So how about we take those two things and merge them together? And so begins GrumpBound, a game for Windows, Linux and Mac. Grump-themed RPG based off Earthbound, where the chosen four - Arin, Dan, Jon and Ross - embark on an adventure to save the world. This game is a present for all the lovelies out there. It's made by the community and for the community. The game will be entirely free to play. It is not a romhack, so you don't have to own a copy of Earthbound before playing. It's made with love with a custom engine - this is not your typical RPG Maker fangame. We are really trying. The project is still under heavy development. Expect a playable demo soon. In the meantime, you can follow us on social media to get notified about any updates. It all depends on you and your support. Help the game happen by sharing it with any lovelies you know. You can also join our team if you want to support us directly.
